Add Kay to your
home screen

Install Kay as an app in seconds — no App Store needed. Works on iPhone and Android.

Open Kay
Choose your device
1
Open Kay in Safari
Tap the Open Kay button above. Make sure you're using Safari — it won't work in Chrome or other browsers on iPhone.
2
Tap the Share button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share button — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up.
Share button — centre of bottom toolbar
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
A menu slides up. Scroll down through the options until you see "Add to Home Screen" and tap it.
Add to Home Screen
4
Tap "Add" to confirm
A preview screen appears showing the Kay icon. Tap Add in the top right corner.
Add
5
Find Kay on your home screen
Go to your home screen — Kay's icon is there. Tap it to open Kay fullscreen, just like a real app.
You're all set!
Kay is now on your home screen. Open it any time to continue learning — your sessions are saved automatically.
1
Open Kay in Chrome
Tap the Open Kay button above. Make sure you're using Chrome for the best experience.
2
Look for the install banner
Chrome may automatically show an "Add Kay to Home Screen" banner at the bottom of the screen. If you see it, tap Install and you're done!
Install
3
No banner? Use the menu instead
If no banner appears, tap the three dots ⋮ in the top right corner of Chrome to open the menu.
Three dots menu — top right
4
Tap "Add to Home screen"
In the menu, find and tap "Add to Home screen".
Add to Home screen
5
Tap "Add" to confirm
A popup appears — tap Add to confirm. Kay's icon will appear on your home screen.
Add
You're all set!
